Health & Wellness Tips

Stay Hydrated

Proper hydration is essential for all bodily functions. Aim for 8-10 glasses of water daily, more if you're active or in hot weather.

Balanced Nutrition

Focus on whole foods: colorful vegetables, lean proteins, whole grains, and healthy fats. Limit processed foods and added sugars.

Regular Movement

Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate exercise weekly. Even short walks can significantly benefit your physical and mental health.
